Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the right courses for me?

Start by identifying what you are passionate about and your career aspirations. Consider the specific skills you want to learn and how they align with your professional goals.

Do I get a cartificate for every course I take?

Yes, upon successful completion of each course, you will receive a certificate of achievement. These certificates serve as a formal recognition of your efforts and the skills you have acquired. They can be added to your resume or LinkedIn profile to showcase your accomplishments and enhance your career opportunities.

How experienced and qualified are the teachers?

Our instructors are highly experienced and qualified professionals in their respective fields. They possess extensive knowledge and practical experience in programming and technology. Many of them hold advanced degrees and certifications, and they have worked in the industry for several years, bringing real-world insights into the classroom.

Will I have lifetime a access to courses I purchase?

Yes, when you purchase a course, you will have lifetime access to it. This means you can revisit the course material, including videos, resources, and updates, at any time, even after you've completed the course. This flexibility allows you to learn at your own pace and revisit topics as needed to reinforce your understanding or refresh your skills.

Will you assist me to get my first job?

Yes, we are dedicated to supporting you in your journey to secure your first job. Our platform offers various resources to help you succeed

What are your payment options?

We offer a variety of payment options to make it convenient for you to enroll in our courses: Credit and Debit Cards: You can pay using major credit and debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. PayPal: For those who prefer using an online payment platform, we accept payments through PayPal. Bank Transfers: We also offer bank transfer options for larger transactions or institutional purchases. Please contact us for more details on this method. Payment Plans: Depending on the course, we may offer installment payment plans to help you manage your budget.

What are the requirements to start?

To begin your learning journey with us, you typically need the following: Basic Computer Skills: Familiarity with using a computer, navigating the internet, and handling files is essential. A Device: You can access our courses using a laptop, desktop computer, or tablet. A stable internet connection is also necessary for streaming videos and participating in online activities. Willingness to Learn: A positive attitude and motivation to learn are crucial for your success in our courses. Course-Specific Prerequisites: Some courses may have specific prerequisites or recommended knowledge. Be sure to check the course description for any requirements related to prior experience or skills. Account Creation: You’ll need to create an account on our platform to enroll in courses. This process is straightforward and only takes a few minutes.

Do I get all sources code from projects?

Yes, upon completing each project in our courses, you will have access to all the source code files. This includes not only the final versions of the projects but also any supplementary files and resources that may help you understand the code better. Having access to the source code allows you to study the implementations, modify the code for your own learning, and use it as a reference for your future projects. We encourage you to experiment with the code and make it your own!
